Seraphyne AI

Prototype

AI-Powered Clinical Decision Intelligence Platform

A clinical simulation platform that strengthens medical decision-making through realistic AI patient conversations, counterfactual analysis, and disease-progression simulation — giving students a safe space to practice diagnostic reasoning while institutions measure learning outcomes.

03

VEOS

Concept

Village Entitlement & Opportunity System

A governance-tech platform that connects eligible rural households to welfare schemes, local jobs, and essential services — built offline-first for low-connectivity regions, with a frozen eligibility roster designed for accountable, SDG-aligned coverage tracking.

Problem solved

Eligible households are excluded from welfare schemes due to documentation gaps, fragmented systems, and no fixed eligibility baseline.

Key learnings

Designing for 2G connectivity and low digital literacy first — and why offline sync and role-based access aren't optional in this context.

  • Welfare Targeting Engine
  • Employment Convergence
  • Offline-First Architecture
  • SDG-Aligned Monitoring
Android SQLite Node.js

04 Featured

PRISM

Concept

Personal Risk & Insight Simulation Model

A preventive healthcare intelligence concept that turns sparse lifestyle and behavioral data — sleep, nutrition, stress, activity — into multiple simulated future health trajectories, so people can act before symptoms appear rather than after.

Problem solved

Most health tracking is retrospective — it shows what already happened instead of forecasting where current habits lead.

Key learnings

Working with incomplete, sparse inputs meant designing for synthesis and confidence scoring rather than assuming complete data.

  • Multi-Trajectory Simulation
  • Predictive Risk Analytics
  • Privacy-First Architecture
  • Rule-Based Decision Support
